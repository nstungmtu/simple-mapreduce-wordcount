summary 
recommendation itu-t y.3518 provides the overview of inter-cloud data management and its functional 
requirements. it describes typical use cases and specifies functional requirements for three aspects, namely 
inter-cloud data policy, inter-cloud data isolation and protection, as well as inter-cloud data management, 
which are derived from the corresponding use cases. 
keywords 
inter-cloud; data management; inter-cloud data policy; inter-cloud data isolation and protection.
