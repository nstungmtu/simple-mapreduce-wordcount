1 framework and requirements for cloud computing  
 
278  
summary 
recommendation itu-t y.3511 describes the framework for interactions of multiple cloud service providers 
(csps), which is referred to as inter -cloud computing. based on several use case, and after considering the 
different types of service offerings, this recommendation describes the possible relationships (peering, 
federation or intermediary) among multiple csps. by introducing the concept of primary csp and secondary 
csp, the recommendation further describes csp interactions for the cases of federation and intermediary 
patterns. finally, relevant functional requirements are derived. 
keywords 
cloud computing, infrastructure, inter-cloud computing, network, primary csp, requirement, secondary csp, 
use case.
