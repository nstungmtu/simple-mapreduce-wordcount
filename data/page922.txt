5 intercloud and interoperability  
 
914  
summary 
supplement 65 to itu-t q-series recommendations provides the summary information for cloud computing 
interoperability activities of existing standards development organizations (sdos) and the groups, forums 
and open sources developing the specifications that have the potential to utilize  cloud computing 
interoperability testing tools. this supplement should also be helpful for the development of cloud testing 
specifications.
