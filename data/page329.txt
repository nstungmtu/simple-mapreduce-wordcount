3.2 terms defined in this recommendation 
this recommendation defines the following terms: 
3.2.1 big data: a paradigm for enabling the collection, storage, management, analysis and visualization, 
potentially under real-time constraints, of extensive datasets with heterogeneous characteristics. 
note â€“ examples of datasets characteristics include high-volume, high-velocity, high-variety, etc. 
3.2.2 big data as a service (bdaas) : a cloud service category in which the capabilities provided to the 
cloud service customer are the ability to collect, store, analyse, visualize and manage data using big data. 
3.2.3 party: natural person or legal person, whether or not incorporated, or a group of either.
